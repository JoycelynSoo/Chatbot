Q: what can i apply for?
A: You can apply for a UTS Social Impact Grant if your project focuses on either:

* **Safe and Inclusive UTS:**  This category supports projects that aim to create a welcoming and supportive campus environment for diverse equity groups, including students from low-SES backgrounds, rural/regional/remote areas, First Nations students and staff, CARM communities, people with disability, LGBTIQA+ communities, and women.

Remember, projects in the "Thriving Communities" category **must** include a community partner, while projects in the "Safe and Inclusive UTS" category do not need a community partner.
